**ZAS-53** is a ZUS form: the *wniosek o zasiłek chorobowy* — an
**application for sickness benefit**. Whether it applies to you depends on
your specific situation. Use the official sources below or contact ZUS to
confirm.

Official ZUS pages mention ZAS-53 in situations where a person applies to ZUS
directly for sickness benefit — for example people running a business, people
whose sickness benefit is paid by ZUS rather than the employer, and situations
**after insurance has ended**, where it appears alongside the **Z-10**
statement.

Practical context from official sources:

- The form is published on the ZUS forms pages and can be filled in
  electronically via **eZUS/PUE** or printed;
- It is a different thing from the **e-ZLA** — the e-ZLA is the *sick note*
  issued by a doctor, while ZAS-53 is an *application* a person submits;
- ZUS pages group it with other sickness-benefit forms such as **Z-3** and
  **Z-10**, each with its own role.

If you have seen ZAS-53 mentioned in your situation, the official form page
below describes what it is for and how it can be submitted; ZUS decides how
any application is handled.
