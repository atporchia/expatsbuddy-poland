**CEIDG** (*Centralna Ewidencja i Informacja o Działalności Gospodarczej*) is
the national register of one-person businesses. Official sources describe
registration as a single online application submitted through **biznes.gov.pl**,
after signing in via login.gov.pl.

That one application generally does more than just create a CEIDG entry.
Official sources describe it as also handling, in the same step:

- notifying the tax office to obtain a **NIP**;
- notifying the statistics office (GUS) to obtain a **REGON**;
- registering you with **ZUS** as a payer of social and health insurance
  contributions.

You do not need to visit each office separately for these — that is the
point of the single CEIDG application, according to official sources.

**Changing your details later** — for example your business address, added
or removed **PKD** codes, or contact information — generally uses the same
CEIDG-1 application, submitted again with the updated information. Official
sources describe this as free of charge, like the initial registration.

For non-EU/EEA/Swiss/US citizens, official sources note that a document
confirming your residence title allows setting up a business is generally
required as part of registration. The official pages below list current
requirements, forms, and any recent procedural changes — check them before
you register or update your details.
